# Water Clean – Motor Mixing Pump Overload

## Issue
- Issue description: Motor mixing pump overload
- Model: SMT1 & SMT2, C8.5 Big & Small
- Machine: EQDIW000021
- Time: 00:30 – 02:40 (03/26/2025)

## Timeline
- 03/26 00:30 → Issue happened
  - Machine alarm: Mixing tank overload
  - Machine could not run automatically
- 03/26 02:30 → EQ troubleshooting
  - EQ repaired mixing motor of mixing tank
  - Mixing process completed
  - Mixing tank could supply solvent to Station No.1 and Station No.2
- 03/26 02:40 → Machine recovered

## Symptom
- Mixing tank overload alarm occurred.
- Automatic operation stopped.
- Mixing motor could not work normally.

## Root Cause
- The rotation shaft became stuck because dirty material built up on the bearing.
- Because the bearing was dirty and stuck, the motor could not rotate normally.
- This caused the motor mixing pump overload condition.

## Failure Mechanism
- The mixing motor depends on normal rotation of the shaft and bearing.
- Dirty buildup on the bearing increased resistance and caused the rotation shaft to stick.
- Because the shaft could not rotate smoothly, motor load increased abnormally.
- That abnormal mechanical load triggered motor mixing pump overload and prevented automatic mixing operation.

## Actions
1. EQ opened the bearing area of the rotation shaft.
2. Cleaned dirty buildup on the bearing.
3. Applied lubrication oil.
4. Restored the motor / mixing-tank function.
5. Added bearing maintenance item into the monthly maintenance check sheet.

## Current Status
- Mixing tank repair was completed.
- Mixing process was completed again.
- Mixing tank could supply solvent to Station No.1 and Station No.2.
- Machine recovered from 02:40.
- Monthly PM item for bearing was added.

## Impact
- Quality: no direct product NG quantity stated in the slide

- Safety: not stated in the report
- Repeat risk: possible if bearing contamination / lubrication condition is not maintained

## Open Risk / Follow-up
- Need to ensure monthly bearing maintenance is sustained
- Need to monitor whether rotation shaft shows stuck tendency again
- Need to verify lubrication condition during routine PM
