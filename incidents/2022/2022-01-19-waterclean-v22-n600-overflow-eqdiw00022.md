# V22 Water Cleaning Machine – Machine Overflow N600

## Issue
- Issue: Machine overflow N600
- Model: C6.5-X1818 Big-Sm
- Machine: EQDIW00022
- Time: 19/01/2022 04:45 AM

## Symptom
- N600 solution overflowed from the machine.
- Liquid leakage was found at the pump motor area.

## Root Cause
- The O-ring sealing between the pump chamber and the outside was damaged.
- Because the O-ring was damaged, N600 liquid leaked out from the pump motor.

## Failure Mechanism
- The pump chamber relies on the O-ring to keep the liquid path sealed from the outside environment.
- Once the O-ring was damaged, sealing integrity was lost.
- N600 solution then leaked from the pump motor area and caused machine overflow.

## Short Action
1. EQ stopped the machine.
2. EQ checked machine status and detected liquid leakage from the pump motor.
3. EQ locked / isolated the pump motor valve.
4. Used anti-spill tool to handle the spilled N600 solution.
5. Cleaned the machine and the spilled N600 area.
6. Repaired and replaced the O-ring on the pump motor shaft.
7. After repair, the machine worked normally from 10:30 AM.
8. To prevent repeat issue on another pump, checked all remaining pump motors and confirmed O-ring condition was normal.

## Long Action
- Add checking status item of the O-ring pumps to the monthly maintenance checklist.

## Current Status
- Machine recovered after repairing and replacing the O-ring on the pump motor shaft.
- Machine worked normally from 10:30 AM.
- Long-term PM checklist item was added.

## Impact
- Quality: not stated in the slide
- Production: temporary machine stop due to chemical leakage
- Safety: N600 overflow / leakage is a chemical handling and machine safety risk
- Repeat risk: reduced after replacing the damaged O-ring and checking remaining pumps, but PM monitoring is still required

## Open Risk / Follow-up
- Need to maintain monthly PM check for pump O-ring status
- Need to monitor remaining pump motors for early seal wear
- Need to confirm no repeat N600 leakage after recovery
