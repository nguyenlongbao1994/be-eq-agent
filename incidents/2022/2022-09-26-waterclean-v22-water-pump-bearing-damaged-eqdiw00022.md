# The Bearing of Water pump was damaged

## Issue
- Issue: The Bearing of Water pump was damaged
- NG q’ty: 0
- Model: C8.5 Small / Big
- Machine: Water clean machine SMT2 (DIW 00022)
- Time: 09:00 AM, 26-Sep-2022

## Symptom
- When starting the water pump of chamber #6, the water pump was very noisy.
- The water pump could not run normally.

## Root Cause Analysis
- The water pump had worked for a long time.
- The bearing and shaft of the motor were damaged.
- This caused abnormal noise and unstable / abnormal pump operation.

## Root Cause
- Long operating time caused damage to the bearing and motor shaft of the water pump.

## Failure Mechanism
- The pump depends on the bearing and shaft to keep rotation stable and aligned.
- After long-time operation, bearing wear increased and shaft damage occurred.
- Once the bearing and shaft were damaged, pump rotation became noisy and unstable.
- Because of this mechanical damage, the water pump of chamber #6 could not run normally.

### Immediate / Short Action
1. Set up water clean SMT1 (DIW 00021) to produce for both SMT1 and SMT2.
2. Contact Trong Hung Vendor to maintain the motor on 9/27.
3. Checkpoint for maintenance follow-up: 9/29.

## Current Status

## Impact

- Repeat risk: possible if pump-life / bearing-life monitoring is not controlled
