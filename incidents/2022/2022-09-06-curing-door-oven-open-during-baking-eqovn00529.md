# Door Oven open During Baking Time

## Issue
- Issue: Door Oven open During Baking Time
- Model: C8.5 Big / Small
- Machine: Curing EQOVN00529
- NG Q’ty: 0
- Time: 16:36 PM (9/6/2022)

## Symptom
- Oven door opened during baking time.
- Issue happened again on 9/10/2022 after double checking with PD team.
- No NG quantity was reported in the slide.

## Root Cause
- The steel key height of the new oven machine (EQOVN00529) was 5 mm lower than the old oven machine.
- Because the steel key height was lower, the contact area between steel key and door lock was not enough.
- When the oven machine worked at high temperature or when there was any impact with the oven door, the steel key could disconnect from the door lock.
- This caused the “Door Oven open during baking time” issue.

## Failure Mechanism
- Door lock stability depends on sufficient engagement between the steel key and the door lock.
- The new oven machine had a steel key installed lower than the old design by approximately 5 mm.
- Because engagement depth / contact area was reduced, door locking became weak.
- Under high temperature or door vibration / impact, the steel key could disengage from the door lock.
- Once disengaged, the oven door opened during baking time.

## Actions

### Short action
- Moved up the installed position of steel key by about 5 mm
- Action done on 9/10/2022
- Oven machine could work normally after adjustment

### Long action
- Requested PT team to make a new steel key
- Checkpoint: 9/12/2022

## Current Status
- Short-term containment by moving the steel key up 5 mm was completed.
- Machine could work normally after adjustment.
- Long-term action for a new steel key was requested.

## Impact
- Quality: NG Q’ty = 0
- Production: baking process reliability affected until adjustment was completed
- Safety: oven door opening during baking is a safety-related equipment issue
- Repeat risk: still possible until the new steel key design / replacement is completed

## Open Risk / Follow-up
- Need PT team to complete the new steel key
- Need to verify new steel key gives enough contact area with door lock
- Need to monitor door-lock stability under high temperature and door impact condition
