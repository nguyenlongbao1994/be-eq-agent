# Attis – EQCPF00094 Glue Valve Error

## Issue

## Symptom

## Root Cause
- The valve holder / collar became loose.
- The screw thread of the holder was damaged.
- Because the holder fastening was damaged, the chamber and heater became loose.
- This loosen condition caused glue to leak from the valve and contaminate the products.

## Failure Mechanism
- Valve sealing and stable dispense condition depend on a secure holder / collar fixation.
- When the holder screw thread was damaged, clamping force could not be maintained.
- The chamber and heater around the valve became loose.
- Once this sealing / holding condition was weakened, glue leaked out from the valve path.
- The leaked glue contaminated the panel surface and triggered the issue.

## Actions
1. Check FAI and valve maintenance records → normal
2. Check time of maintenance and setup valve → normal
3. Quickly remove the failure valve
4. Inspect holder / collar and confirm loosen condition
5. Inspect holder screw thread and confirm damage
6. Replace another holder for the valve

## Preventive Action
- Add holder screw check in maintenance list
